Thoracic surgery

Overview

Thoracic surgery provides services for the surgical treatment of non-cardiac, benign and malignant diseases of the chest. The Thoracic Surgery specialists at Max Hospital, offer consultation and surgical management of a wide range of disorders and diseases involving: Lungs Pleura (Chest cavity) Trachea and bronchus (wind pipe) Esophagus (food pipe) Chest wall Diaphragm Mediastinum Chest Trauma Hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating): of axilla or hand
